Product Category

 
Batch Orchestrator may have multiple product categories depending upon the user’s requirement. Each of the product category will manage different formulation to develop different products.

Create Product Category

Below we discuss the steps to create Product Categories in Batch Orchestrator:

  1. Click on “Create New”




  2. It will take you to a new window where the list of all the Master Recipes in Experion used for product development is available.




  3. Select the desired recipe and click on “Import”.




  4. This will import all the essential formula parameters, defined in the data block of your selected master recipe.




  5. Process Engineer here, whose role is to manage the formula sets, can review the information imported as part of the formula parameter. You can define each of these parameter as raw materials or ingredients for your product.




    6. NOTE:This raw material information will be used as part of campaign manager, to be introduced in next release.


  6. You product category is now created, as shown below in the image:




  7. Further you can edit icon, assign keyword as per your requirements to help you identify the Product easily:




  8. To delete the product category, you can simply use “Delete Product Category” from the above action items.
