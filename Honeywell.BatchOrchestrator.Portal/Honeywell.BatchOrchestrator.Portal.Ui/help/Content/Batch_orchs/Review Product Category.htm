Approve / Review Product Categories

 
Once you have the product category or formula set created, you can review and proceed with approvals for it:




Batch Orchestrator has a strategic approval mechanism. To approve a product category or formula set follow below steps:
  1. Go to the main menu as shown below, and select “Approvals”:




  2. You will be directed to window, where all the pending approvals are accessible for review”




  3. Select the Product Category, you can review all the details of the product category including whether raw materials selected are correct or not.




  4. Once you have confirmed that all the detail and information is correct, click on the “Approve“. Similarly, you can reject or add your comments to the selected product category as well.
