Create Products

 

Once the product category is approved, Process Engineer can start with defining different products for it. A Product Category or Formula Set may have different types of product, where their base formulation is same under the product category but are different variants of it as part of manufacturing process.




  1. Click on the icon “Create Product”:




  2. You will directed to the window where you can define a product as part of your manufacturing process.
    1. Product Name: Name of the product for market launch
    2. Product ID: To be linked with SAP to manage product in future.
    3. Version: Not supported as part of R100
    4. Description: Details about the product.

      5. NOTE: You can select “Parameter Description” to define the formulation of your product.
    5. Value: Change amount as per your requirement of the respective parameter
    6. Engineer Unit: Pre defined and fixed for each parameter
    7. Minimum Value: Enable you to decide the right range over the optimum range to manufacture a product.
    8. Maximum Value: Enable you to decide the right range over the optimum range to manufacture a product.
    9. Add Comments; Save Draft; Submit for Approval: Action items for the product to decribe, save and submit for approval
    10. Cancel: If you need to discard the product completely.





  3. Once you are done editing the product, it will appear in the product category with status as draft:




  4. Once the product is created, you can submit it for approval:




  5. The product status will now move to review:




Once the product is created and reviewed, user can now proceed to approving the product and product categories.
